What we do

Ahava utilizes aeroponic systems to maximize growth potential and product quality.

Suspension

Plant roots are suspended in the air, allowing for maximum oxygen intake.

Nutrient Misting

A nutrient-rich solution is misted directly onto the roots, providing essential nutrients without drowning the plant.

Controlled Environment

Light, temperature, and humidity are precisely controlled to optimize growth.

Continuous Monitoring

Advanced sensors and automated systems monitor the growing conditions 24/7

Benefits of Aeroponics

Maximize your product's potential and unlock new possibilities.

Enhanced Potency

Aeroponic cannabis often has higher cannabinoid and terpene levels, delivering stronger effects and richer flavors.

Purity and Cleanliness

Grown in a soilless environment, our cannabis is free from soil-borne contaminants and pests.

Consistent Quality

The controlled environment ensures uniform growth and quality across all plants.

Eco-Friendly

Uses up to 90% less water and fewer resources compared to traditional growing methods.
